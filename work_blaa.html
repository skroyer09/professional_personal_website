BLAAGAARD TEATER

In Theme 8, our task was to assist Blaagaard Teater, who wanted us to investigate how theaters as an institution can attract an audience that is not normally interested in the local theater scene. To achieve this, the theater wanted os to find new ways to make their performances more exciting for potential new customers and thus attract more people to their theatre.

This involved redesigning their website and creating a commercial video to promote their work.
