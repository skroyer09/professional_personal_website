NintendoPusher

In Theme 10, we had the opportunity to work with Nintendopusher, a company seeking to update its online presence to better reflect its brand and enhance the user experience for its customers. Our task was to assist them in updating their visual identity on their existing website, which involved developing a more modern and cohesive design that aligned with their brand values and mission.

Moreover, we were also tasked with improving the overall user experience by streamlining the website's navigation and functionality. This included enhancing the site's search capabilities and making it easier for customers to find what they were looking for quickly.

Additionally, we worked on a more efficient social media strategy that would help the company attract and retain a larger customer base. This involved analyzing their target audience, creating engaging content that aligned with their brand, and identifying the best social media platforms to reach their audience.
