RikkeRavn Design

In Theme 5, our task was as a group to redesign a company's website to improve the user experience. My role was to establish contact with the company and schedule interviews and filming as well as to code for the redesigned website.

I contacted the company via phone and email to create a good dialogue and was able to easily schedule meetings and ask questions about their wishes for the redesign.

This course has taught me the importance of having good contact both with the customer and with one's team. Good communication is the key to a successful project and it is important to keep all parties involved updated and informed about the process.
