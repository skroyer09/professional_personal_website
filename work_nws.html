NewWave Shirts

In Theme 3, we focused on UX design, which involves understanding the interaction between users and user interfaces, and conducting research and analysis to improve the user experience.

I personally conducted a test of my prototype using a five act interview, which helped me identify issues with navigating the interface. To address these issues, I implemented a hover-state on the buttons that allowed users to move forward, and made the main menu interactive to facilitate navigation.

This course has taught me the importance of conducting thorough research and analysis to create a more effective and user-friendly interface.
